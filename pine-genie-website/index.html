Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pine Genie

How does Pine Genie work?

+

Pine Genie uses advanced AI algorithms like Simulated Annealing and Genetic Algorithms to automatically test thousands of parameter combinations for your TradingView strategies, finding the optimal settings in minutes instead of hours.

Is my data secure?

+

Yes, absolutely. Pine Genie runs entirely in your browser with encrypted local storage. No strategy data is sent to external servers. Your trading strategies and results remain completely private.

Can I use it with any Pine Script strategy?

+

Pine Genie works with any Pine Script strategy on TradingView. Simply open your strategy, and Pine Genie will automatically detect optimizable parameters and help you find the best settings.

How much faster is it than manual optimization?

+

Pine Genie is typically 10-50x faster than manual parameter testing. What would take hours or days manually can be completed in minutes with our AI algorithms.

Do I need programming knowledge?

+

No programming knowledge required! Pine Genie has an intuitive interface. Just set your parameter ranges, choose an algorithm, and click start. The extension handles all the complex optimization automatically.

What's included in the free trial?

+

The free trial includes 10 optimizations per day with basic algorithms and CSV export. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited optimizations, 3D visualization, and AI suggestions.
